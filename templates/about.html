What is BJJWeb.com?

BJJWeb.com is designed to be the definitive source for Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu techniques and tactics on the web.

Why would I use BJJWeb.com?

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is unbelievably complicated! Every time I watch an advanced match or see someone catch their opponent with a slick trap it blows my mind. I made BJJWeb.com to help me manage that complexity by organizing the art and displaying it visually. Hopefully this helps you too!

How do I use BJJWeb.com?

Browse the Techniques section to see classes of techniques, which are linked to public Youtube videos that can help you learn them. In order to visualize the art and find new strategies, use the Flows section where you can see how everything fits together. your game and fill in gaps, use the Flows section

What is a Flow?

A BJJWeb.com "Flow" is a set of techniques that are grouped together, for whatever reason, and displayed as a flow chart. Some examples of grouping could be: all collar chokes, advanced traps, De la Riva Guard passes, or Ronda Rousey's armbar setups.

What are some ways I could use BJJWeb.com Flows?

  • Map your own game to discover gaps
  • Map your favorite player's game and compare it to others
  • Map an opponent's game and explore an arsenal of technqiues to attack them
  • etc...!
